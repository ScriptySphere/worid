Common Questions

How do I report a fraudulent listing?
Click the "Report" button on any listing page, or email us at safety@buysellbd.com.bd. Our Trust & Safety team investigates all reports within 2 hours.
How long does it take to get my listing approved?
Most listings are reviewed and approved within 30 minutes. During peak hours (evenings and weekends), it may take up to 2 hours.
I paid for an item but didn't receive it. What do I do?
Contact us immediately at support@buysellbd.com.bd with your order details. Our dispute resolution team will step in and help mediate within 24 hours.
Can I advertise my business on BuySell Bangladesh?
Yes! We offer featured listing packages, banner advertisements, and category sponsorships. Email business@buysellbd.com.bd for our media kit.
