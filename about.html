Our Story

We started BuySell Bangladesh with a simple dream — to make buying and selling easy, safe, and accessible for every Bangladeshi, from Teknaf to Tetulia.

Who We Are

Built for Bangladesh, by Bangladeshis

Founded in 2020, BuySell Bangladesh was born from the idea that commerce should be simple, trusted, and local. We saw millions of Bangladeshis struggling to find reliable platforms to buy and sell — so we built one.

Today, we are proud to serve over 850,000 users across all 64 districts of Bangladesh, with more than 2.4 million active listings in 16 major categories. From a small apartment in Dhaka, we have grown into the country's most trusted marketplace.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower every Bangladeshi — from rural farmers to urban entrepreneurs — with a safe, simple, and powerful platform to buy, sell, and trade goods and services. We believe commerce should be accessible to all, regardless of location, education, or income level.

Our Vision

To become South Asia's most trusted regional marketplace — where every transaction is safe, every seller is verified, and every buyer finds exactly what they need. We envision a digitally connected Bangladesh where the economy thrives through technology.

Our Values

Trust. Transparency. Innovation. We operate with zero tolerance for fraud and ensure every interaction on our platform meets the highest ethical standards. We put our users — buyers and sellers alike — at the center of every decision we make.

Our Commitment

We are committed to building a safer digital marketplace. Our fraud detection, seller verification, and payment protection systems work 24/7 to ensure every deal is legitimate. Your trust is our most valuable asset.
