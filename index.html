FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a free ad on BuySell Bangladesh?
Simply click "Sell Now", fill in your product details, upload photos and submit. Your listing will be live after a quick review — usually within 30 minutes.
Is BuySell Bangladesh free to use?
Yes! Posting basic ads is completely free. We offer premium featured listings for sellers who want more visibility at a small fee.
How do I stay safe when buying or selling?
Always meet in public places, verify the item before paying, use our secure payment options (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), and report any suspicious activity to our support team.
What payment methods are supported?
We support bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and bank transfers. Always use a traceable payment method for your protection.
Can I sell outside my city?
Absolutely! Your listing is visible across all of Bangladesh. You can arrange nationwide delivery through SA Parcel, Sundarban Courier, or Pathao.
